Chapter 1: Data, Information, and Knowledge

Overview

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of data, information, and knowledge in the context of Information and Communication Technology.

Key Topics

  • Data: Raw facts, figures, and symbols without context
  • Information: Processed data with meaning and value
  • Knowledge: Understanding gained from information to make decisions
  • Characteristics: Persistence, reproducibility, and easy spreading
  • Information Types: Primary vs Secondary sources
  • Data Processing: Converting raw data into meaningful information
